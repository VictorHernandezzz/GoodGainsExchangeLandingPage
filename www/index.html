What is Play 2 Earn?

Play 2 Earn is a truly decentralized gaming ecosystem where gamers are in charge of their own economies. No more worry about getting stripped or shut down because you're not at fault! You own all your in-game items until you decide otherwise, so have fun trading these digital assets within our platform!

So, in play 2 earn games you can own all of your in-game items?

Yup! In this game ecosystem everything is digital - it's not like reality where people might be able to claim their property back after selling on another marketplace. But don't worry because our blockchain technology allows for an exciting new way to play-to earn or stake your digital assets instead.

What is GoodGains Exchange?

GoodGains is a fully immersive digital exchange where all items and skins are owned by the players. You can sell them on any digital assets marketplace or use them within your own game to show off your unique style and taste to new players!

Additionally, this model provides gamers with ownership of in-game assets and continues increasing their value as much as players stay active!

Blockchain games are a new way to experience gaming. Instead of playing with worthless gaming assets, you get items that are scarce and can only be obtained by buying them or trading for them on a digital marketplace such as GoodGains Exchange - this creates more value than ever before for investors and gamers alike!

Now there are many ways to earn crypto while playing! You can choose whether it's through gameplay, investing in game assets and even selling items within your video game of choice. So start playing and earning today! Welcome to GoodGains, Welcome to next level investing.

How GoodGains is Working with Game Developers

Game Development is an art form that we at GoodGains are looking to help support. In the future, we will take a percentage of our earnings yearly to fund indie game developers in the crypto play to earn space.

If you have a game under development that you would like help with we can offer financial funding. We want quality games that add to the gaming experience and bring something new to the table. With our help, your game could be one of those titles!

The Benefits of this Partnership

A partnership with GoodGains and game developers can provide a number of benefits. For one, we can help to automate smart contract tasks and reduce the need for intermediaries. We will utilize the GGU token as a means of transactions for game developers that want to integrate into our ecosystem. This can help to speed up transactions and reduce costs. In addition, GoodGains can help to enforce rules and regulations, ensuring that all parties adhere to the terms of the agreement. Finally, GoodGains can provide a high degree of security and transparency.

How GoodGains can be used in the P2E Space

Blockchain-based games are all the rage these days, and for good reason. Not only are they incredibly fun and addictive, but they also offer players a chance to earn cryptocurrency.

One of the most popular games in this genre is CryptoKitties. The premise of the game is simple: players collect and breed digital cats, using smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. These cats can then be traded or sold for Ethereum's native currency, ETH.

We want to take it to the next level...

Imagine if you could list your NFT’s on Goodgains, or swap your other ERC coins directly to GGX/GGU and then swap our coin for other cryptograms, and crypto assets.

If you are a game developer, we would love to hear from you. We are always looking for new and innovative games to add to our platform. If you have a game that you think would be a good fit for GoodGains, please do not hesitate to get in touch. We look forward to hearing from you soon!

How do NFT Rentals Work?

Think of rentals as a way for gamers to play with the non-fungible tokens used in blockchain games, which often come with an Hefty price. We took it upon ourselves to lower the barrier to entry to new players to make play 2 earn accessible to everyone! We’re also putting a cap on the amount lenders can charge to protect renters as well. This enables more players than ever before in the play to earn space. In exchange for renting these NFTs and paying back your lender some of the crypto earned from playing or farming/racing online; you get access like never before to the blockchain space!

How will the cap on pricing work?

The cap will be based on the amount of income generated per day for playing. For example, if you rent a digital asset(NFT) for 50 coins for said blockchain game, you would generate 150 coins of that game if you played for a long enough period of time in a 24 hour period (the average rental period used on our platform).. This is designed to make our platform profitable for both lenders and renters alike. Welcome to Next level investing, Welcome to GoodGains Exchange.
